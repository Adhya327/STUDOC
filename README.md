[S] Studoc — Academic Vault


"Knowledge is the only vault that grows richer the more you share from it."



Studoc is a student-focused web platform for organizing academic documents, building professional resumes, and connecting with a community of driven, intellectually curious students.


What is Studoc?

Students lose notes. Files scatter across devices. Group chats get buried. Studoc fixes that.

It gives every student a clean, organized digital space to store their academic work — accessible anytime, from any device. Alongside storage, Studoc includes a resume builder and a community forum where students help, guide, and inspire each other.


Features

Document Vault


Upload any file — PDF, DOCX, PPTX, images, and more
Drag & drop support for instant uploads
Auto-categorization by subject (Science, Math, Humanities, Engineering)
Search and filter your documents instantly
Delete files you no longer need


Resume Builder


Fill in your academic profile — name, university, degree, skills, projects
Live preview panel that generates a clean, styled resume in real time
Copy or export your resume for job and internship applications


Student Community


Post thoughts, questions, or resources for fellow students
Tag posts by topic — Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics, Career Advice, Resources
Like posts, reply to threads, and share content
Filter the feed by topic to find relevant discussions
See how many students are active right now


General


Smooth page transitions with animated UI
Toast notifications for every action
Fully responsive — works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
Dark scholarly theme — easy on the eyes during late-night study sessions
